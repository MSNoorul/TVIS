About SSC

SSC was founded in 2017 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Over the years, we have grown steadily by maintaining our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We specialize in high-quality pumps and complete pump systems from trusted global manufacturers, tailored to diverse customer needs.

Vision

To be a trusted pump supplier known for quality, innovation, and smart investments that support long-term reliability and service excellence.

Mission

To deliver reliable, high-quality pump solutions with exceptional service and technical support, based on trusted products and lasting partnerships.

Strategic Strength

Strong global partnerships empower us with rapid sales, technical, and electrical support, especially for critical projects where operational continuity is essential.

Products & Applications

We supply a broad range of pump technologies and systems for industrial, municipal, residential, and infrastructure use.

Industries We Serve

  • Industrial Operations
  • Residential & Domestic Applications
  • Agricultural, Irrigation & Filter Systems
  • Building Services & Civil Infrastructure

Services

Inspection, Installation & Commissioning

Periodic inspections, proper installation, and full testing & commissioning by experienced specialists to ensure safe and efficient operation.

Maintenance & On-Site Support

Preventive and corrective maintenance, fast response teams, and workshop repair services for all pump types with focus on reducing downtime.

Control Panel Solutions

Design and build of custom control panels including VFD/VSD, Star-Delta, PLC-based control, and fire pump panel solutions (UL/FM & non-UL/FM).

Electro-Mechanical Supply

Comprehensive portfolio of electrical and mechanical products, including breakers, contactors, cables, pipes, valves, flanges, fittings, fasteners, and accessories.
